Classic Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

    2 tbsp olive oil


    1 large onion, finely chopped


    3–4 garlic cloves, minced


    500g ground beef (or beef+pork)


    400g canned tomatoes


    2 tbsp tomato paste


    1 tsp dried oregano


    1 tsp dried basil


    Salt and pepper to taste


    Optional: pinch of sugar


For the Cheese Filling:

250g ricotta cheese


1 egg


½ cup grated Parmesan cheese


Salt and pepper


Others:

    9–12 lasagna noodles (boiled or oven-ready)


    2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese


    Extra Parmesan for topping


    Fresh basil (optional)


Instructions:

    Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    Heat oil, sauté onions & garlic, add meat, cook. Add tomatoes, paste, herbs, salt. Simmer 20–25 mins.


    Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    Mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, salt, and pepper in a bowl.


    Cook the Noodles:
    Boil if not oven-ready. Drain and set aside.


    Assemble Lasagna:
    Layer sauce → noodles → ricotta → sauce → mozzarella. Repeat. Top with cheese.


    Bake:
    Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25 mins. Uncover and bake 15 more mins.


    Rest and Serve:
    Let sit for 10 mins. Garnish with basil.
